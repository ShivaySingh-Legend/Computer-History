History of Computers

Introduction

The computer has evolved from a simple calculator to a powerful tool that runs modern life. Let’s explore its history through time.

Timeline

  • 1801 – Punch cards used in Jacquard loom
  • 1837 – Charles Babbage designs the Analytical Engine
  • 1936 – Alan Turing introduces the concept of the Turing Machine
  • 1945 – ENIAC becomes the first general-purpose computer
  • 1971 – Intel 4004: The first microprocessor
  • 1980s – Personal computers rise (Apple, IBM)
  • 2000s+ – AI, smartphones, and cloud computing shape the modern era
